About This Game


Battle Teams 2 is a fast-paced multiplayer tactical shooter with Asian aesthetics. PvP offers action-packed game modes set in a rigorously balanced competitive gameplay environment. Narrative-driven cooperative PvE gameplay throws you and your friends into wave after wave of merciless adversaries.

Fast-paced, Fair PvP


Battle Teams 2 is dead set to get your blood pumping, whether you favor classic shooter modes like Battle Royale, Bomb Defusal and Team Deathmatch, Free for All, or innovative gameplay like Big Head Hero and Infected. Immerse yourself in fast-paced PvP showdowns where every second is a matter of life or death. No gameplay advantage can be paid for - the playing field is level from start to finish. Your only salvation is your trigger finger.

Thrilling Narratives, Intense Challenges


Team up with your friends and other players to take on bloodthirsty beasts and unforgiving bosses. Clutch victory from the jaws of defeat with your unique abilities and resources. Upgrade your weapons and carry your team into ever darker realms. Uncover the shrouded truth of an otherworldly realm as the story unfolds through exquisitely crafted cinematics.

Battle In Your Way


Discover a diverse arsenal of weapons, each with innumerable customization, modification and enhancement options. Tweak your weapons to suit your playstyle and build loadouts to master all battlefields. Every encounter is uniquely yours.

Optimized For Any PC Specifications


Optimized to run seamlessly on PC's of varying performance capabilities, Battle Teams 2 ensures flexibility and accessibility for everyone. With five distinct graphics settings ranging from very low to very high, the game provides a smooth and enjoyable experience on a wide range of player hardware. Choose the graphics level that best suits your system.

Get Ready for Regional Tournaments


Battle Teams 2 is coming to your region and regional tournaments are not far behind - get ready to join the action! Teams that have proven themselves in regional tournaments will have the chance to face off against global counterparts, showcasing their skills on the global stage.
