About This Content

BlazBlue Entropy Effect - Rachel Character Pack


BlazBlue Entropy Effect Rachel Character Pack includes the following content:
  • New Prototype: Rachel.
She performs attacks with the aid of her familiars. She can also summon lightning and wind to dance gracefully through the battlefield.


  • A set of Prototype Palettes applicable to all characters (including future IP characters).


  • A new exclusive Avatar trail effect.



    Special Note:
    • Players who purchase the base game BlazBlue Entropy Effect (including Early Access) before February 14, 2024, 10:00 (UTC+8) will automatically receive this DLC without needing to purchase it again.
    • Please be aware that this game contains visual elements such as flashing screens that may trigger photosensitive epilepsy.
