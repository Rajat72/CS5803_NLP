About This Game

“CONQUEROR'S BLADE” - THE ULTIMATE EPIC ACTION & TACTICS MMO

As a legendary hero, lead powerful troops and conquer vast territories in epic PvP battles.

Whether you’re a fan of massive siege warfare, brutal action combat, or strategic RTS gameplay, “Conqueror’s Blade” offers an unparalleled experience that will test your mettle and tactical prowess like no other game.

Command Units

Muster over a hundred historically-inspired units, including Vikings, Knights, Chinese dynasties, Samurais, Winged Hussars, Janissaries, Assassins, and more.

Utilize unique unit abilities such as healing, charging, bracing, buffs, and explosives to gain an advantage. New units and maps are added regularly, so adjust your tactics to dominate the battlefield.

Fight as a Hero

Customize your character and wield a range of powerful weapon classes to express your own unique playstyle and build.

Each class can be switched at any time and boasts distinct attacks, special abilities, perks and upgrades, offering unique tactical advantages and combat styles.

Join Open World Territory Wars

Pledge your blade in massive PvP events fought between hundreds of players in Houses and Factions.

Conquer, control and upgrade territories, craft war supplies, and set tax rates to earn weekly revenue for your House. Prepare for climactic Territory Wars showdowns at the end of every Season.

Become a Legend

Territory Wars: Each season, one champion House per server will emerge victorious, earning exclusive rewards and the coveted champion status.

Ranked Ladder: Test your skills in Ranked mode and ascend the leaderboards. Achieve high ranks to earn exclusive rewards and recognition.

E-sports: Compete in the prestigious “Conqueror’s Blade League,” an annual tournament featuring over 1000 participants and prize pools worth tens of thousands of dollars. Rise to the top and claim your glory.

Mature Content Description

The developers describe the content like this:

Gameplay consists of graphic and realistic-looking depictions of physical conflict, weapons and depictions of human injury and death.
