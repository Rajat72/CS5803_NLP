Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: Games in Early Access are not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“In Early Access, your active involvement shapes the development of Gray Zone Warfare. At MADFINGER Games, we are eager to listen to our community's feedback and align with your expectations and preferences. We aim to create an environment that compels you to visit and engage with us daily. Let's make the ultimate tactical FPS experience together.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Gray Zone Warfare is a long-term project that will continue to evolve until we deliver the ultimate gaming experience. We will release regular updates featuring new content, expanding world-building, adding new weapons and gear, unveiling new quests and environmental storytelling, as well as optimizing and fixing bugs before concluding the Early Access phase. The approximate timeline may span several years, depending on community feedback and the fulfillment of our shared vision.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“In the full version, expect an immersive tactical FPS sandbox where you must fully adapt to your surroundings and utilize tactics to your advantage.

The planned state of the Full Release includes:

● A complete map featuring all locations and diverse biomes
● A hazardous Ground Zero area with end-game enemies and new storylines
● Different types of AI behaviors based on the enemy type, situation, time, or weather condition
● Faction-based AI with its own agenda involving quests, patrolling, scavaging, and attacking others
● Factions featuring progression systems and diverse reputations
● Immersive environmental storytelling featuring mature and engaging plots
● Advanced quest system encompassing both main and side storylines
● Game-changing, captivating seasonal events
● Resource-intensive and survival-focused crafting
● Dynamic weather changing the gameplay experience
● An accelerated day and night cycle
● Unique skills system based on the player's achievements, not grind
● Customizable "hooch" player's base quarters
● Enhanced weapon customizations
● Fully customizable gear
● Complex trading system for players

... and other new features sparked by the input and desires of the community.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The Early Access version features an immersive tactical FPS sandbox experience. Look at the "About This Game" section to learn what the game already offers. We plan to enhance this foundation with additional content and feature updates continually.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“Yes. The price of the Early Access version will be lower than that of the final release. The final pricing has yet to be determined.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“All community members are welcome to share feedback, reports, comments, and suggestions on the Gray Zone Warfare Discord:

As an Early Access member, you have the unique opportunity to help us build an excellent foundation for what we envision as a long-lasting, immersive tactical FPS experience.”

About This Game

Following a mysterious event on a fictional Southeast Asian island, the entire area has been placed under international quarantine. With most of the civilian population evacuated under the supervision of the United Nations, three PMC factions are tasked by their enigmatic clients to explore the region, uncover its secrets, and retrieve anything of value.

DEPLOY IN A LIVING OPEN-WORLD

Dive into the ultimate extraction shooter set in a vast 42km² open world of the Democratic Republic of Lamang, inspired by the stunning landscapes of Laos. As a PMC operator, embark on tactical missions to complete tasks, secure high-value loot, and survive intense FPS firefights against rival factions and AI enemies.

SURVIVE REALISTIC COMBAT

Prepare for an immersive combat experience where precise real-world ballistics make every shot count. A unique health system simulates the human body, with injuries affecting perception and performance, forcing you to diagnose wounds and apply the right treatments to survive and support your team.

UNRAVEL A MATURE STORY

Immerse yourself in a mature story experience as you uncover the mysteries of Lamang. Through numerous missions, encounter a colorful cast of morally complex characters, each hiding secrets and pursuing their own agendas. Navigate a web of lies, decide who to trust, and expose the truth in a world of shifting loyalties and gray morality.

THRIVE WITH YOUR ARSENAL

Create your ideal firearm by customizing hundreds of parts, mounts, and accessories to suit your playstyle perfectly. Adapt your tactical gear to tackle any challenge and unforeseen situations. In this high-stakes environment, every move matters, and every fight could result in losing your hard-earned gear and progress.
