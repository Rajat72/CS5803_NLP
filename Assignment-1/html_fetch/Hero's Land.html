About This Game

Game Info

Embark on a journey across a continent riddled with danger, where formidable monsters and fierce enemies lurk around every corner. Here, survival is a brutal game of might and cunning, with Heroes fighting to upgrade their weapons and gear and enhance their chances in the relentless fight for existence. Your accomplishments are proudly displayed on the leaderboards for all to see, but remember to be on guard when others seek to usurp your rank. But the question remains: How will you carve your path to survival?


Endless resources and mobs await outside the safety zone. Equip your finest build from stash and inventory, selling loot to level up fast. Hack 'n' Slash your way out!


Survive and thrive with the hero of your choice. Take on mobs and bosses to loot epic weapons and gear. Alawys be prepared for PvP situations that may break out at any time.


Heroes will face substantial losses of levels upon death, with the peril of losing your gear and weapons on treacherous maps. Yet, the riskiest zones yield the most incredible rewards—the longer you last, the greater your potential gains. Stake it all to secure the best gear unlock heroes, pets, and talents, making you even more formidable in your next fight!


Dive into the action with our game's rich lineup of PVP events, from battle royales to guild wars. Tailor your inventory on the fly with various weapons and gear to suit every twist and turn of battles. Leverage unique buildings like Boost Pads, Sentries, Traps, and more to outmaneuver opponents. Create your own combination of survival and destruction!


Team up in dungeon raids and other engaging game modes, or ally with friends to build a guild and reign supreme. Plus, bring up to three pets, each with unique skills, to bolster your strength and strategy on every adventure.
