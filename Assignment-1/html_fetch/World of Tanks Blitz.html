About This Game

It's World of Tanks Blitz, a legendary action-adventure tank game. Blitz is not about war, but tanks! Join this action-packed PvP shooter and explore unique vehicles and a variety of battlefields. Experience the adrenaline rush of intense tank battles that test your skills and strategy.



This FREE tank game keeps you engaged and brings everyday challenges and unique events.

UPGRADE YOUR TANKS

Choose from more than 400 vehicles, ranging from Tier I to sophisticated Tier X machines.

WIN REWARDS

Enjoy in-game events inspired by monsters, dinosaurs, and other game universes! Complete your special missions for rewards to upgrade your favorite vehicles.

FIGHT IN 30+ BATTLE ARENAS

Discover a variety of battlefield arenas with their unique features and machines. Avoid the heat in a sunbaked desert, study ancient ruins in the Mediterranean, or crawl through a dark and dim secret base lost in time.

PLAY IN A TEAM AND WIN TOURNAMENTS

You never play alone! Join clans and play with friends! Build your own platoon, roll into battle as a team, and compete for glory, high rankings, or prizes!

DRIVE HISTORICAL VEHICLES

Drive and upgrade historically accurate tanks and tank destroyers from Germany, France, Japan, Great Britain, China, the USSR, the USA, and other nations.

OPTIMIZED GFX FOR ANY DEVICE

This PvP shooter is automatically optimized for your device. Go to the settings of WOTB to find the balance between fantastic visuals and high FPS.

Start your journey now!
