About This Game

Aimlabs is the #1 aim trainer, trusted by over 40 million players! Are you ready to upgrade your aiming skills?

Aimlabs is a training platform to help sharpen up your core FPS aiming skills. Aimlabs offers a comprehensive set of tools to improve your aim, regardless of which FPS you play. It provides curated training scenarios that will turn you into an aim God in no time.

Dive into specifically designed training exercises to master the art of flicking, tracking and switching. Aimlabs’ detailed analysis feedback gives you valuable insights and guides you towards rapid improvement.

Aimlabs is continuously updated with new features to ensure compatibility with a wide range of games. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned pro, Aimlabs will help to give you a competitive edge.

Features:

  • Aim Training: Master aim fundamentals with tasks that train flicking, tracking, and switching!

  • Game Specific Training: Find the best training playlists tailored for your FPS game of choice!

  • Universal Sensitivity Finder: Find your optimal sensitivity, for any game!

  • Valorant Training Center: Train fundamental VALORANT mechanics like crosshair placement on 1:1 map recreations!

  • Adaptive Tasks: Play every task at your optimal difficulty level to personalize your aim training!

  • Aimlabs Academy: Learn how to apply your training to your favorite FPS games with step-by-step masterclasses hand-crafted by the experts. New lessons drop every month!

  • Discovery AI Coach: Get aim advice from your own personal AI aim coach!

  • Aim Analysis: Reveal your aim strengths + weaknesses with detailed performance feedback!

  • Sensitivity Matcher: Aim train on the exact same sensitivity you use in your FPS games!

  • Ranked Seasons: Benchmark your aim and earn your Aimlabs Rank to see how you compare against other aimers!

  • Leaderboards: Compete against others and show off how good your aim is!

  • Player Customization: Customize your aim training with weapon skins, avatars, banners, titles, and more!

  • Battlepass: Earn exclusive rewards by playing Aimlabs every season!

  • Replays: Watch and learn from how others aim with replays of every task played!

  • Playlist Of The Day: Earn Aim Credits while you train to redeem for awesome rewards, everyday!

  • Customizable Environments: Take complete control of your training environment!

  • Mouse + Controller support: Train your way, regardless of your input device!

  • Player Streaks: Track your training progress by playing every day! 

  • Continuous Updates: New features will be added as we create innovative ways to improve your aim. We’re always adding fresh tasks and content to ensure compatibility with a wide range of games.
