Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: Games in Early Access are not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“We chose Early Access to make our community a part of the development process. With your feedback and suggestions, we aim to make the game more fun and polished. Your contributions during this phase will help us make important updates and improvements as we work towards the full release.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We plan to keep our game in early access for approximately 6 months to 1 year. However, this timeframe may not always be exact. Depending on the feedback from the community and the new features we plan to add, this period may vary. Our goal is to make the game as good as possible before moving to the full release.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The full version is planned to include more content, features, and improvements based on feedback we receive during Early Access. We aim to expand the overall scope of the game by adding new gameplay elements, refining mechanics, and enhancing the user experience. While we have a clear vision for what we’d like to achieve, the specifics may evolve as we continue development. Our priority is to create a polished and enjoyable experience.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Fast Food Simulator's early access version includes basic restaurant management such as taking orders, serving drinks, making burgers, as well as important features such as character and restaurant customization. Players can experience a significant portion of the game, but we continue to add new content and evaluate feedback. Our top priority is to improve important features and existing content with feedback from the community.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The sale price may increase depending on the size of the content to be added.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We plan to involve the community in our development process by actively seeking feedback and suggestions from players. During Early Access, we'll be engaging with our community through forums, social media, and in-game feedback tools to understand your experiences and preferences. This input will help us prioritize updates, fix issues, and introduce new features that enhance the overall gameplay. Our goal is to create a collaborative environment where player feedback directly influences the development and improvement of the game.”

About This Game

Play solo or co-op with up to 6 players. Open your single-player session to other players and friends to play together. Or you can join a game from the lobby and start serving fast food with others via voice and text chat.

Handle everything it takes to keep a fast food joint running smoothly. Experience the realistic operation of authentic drink machines, grills, and fryers.

Explore the chaotic nature of fast-food restaurants where unexpected and bizarre events can occur at any moment, challenging you and your restaurant crew to navigate through teamwork.

Managing your time wisely is critical as you prioritize tasks to keep customers happy and the line moving smoothly. Team coordination is key, so delegate tasks effectively to ensure seamless operations. Get ready to take on the fast-paced world of fast food.
