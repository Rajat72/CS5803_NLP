Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: Games in Early Access are not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Our goal with SMITE 2 has always been to make SMITE, but better. We believe the only true way to achieve this is with ongoing feedback from our community as they know the game best. Early Access is the best fit for this format of game development.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We expect SMITE 2 to be in Early Access development at least through early 2025. Given the nature of Early Access development, we don't have a specific end date planned at this time.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“We expect the full version of SMITE 2 to include a more-complete, more-polished set of features, a more fleshed out god roster, more game modes and content in comparison to the Early Access version.

During Early Access, our plan is to release a Major Update every month containing new and fan favorite gods, new content and more, as well as patches and balance updates as we respond to player feedback.

Early Access is available as a free-to-play title with additional cosmetic benefits available for players who buy a Founder’s Edition. Founder's Editions are expected to remain available for purchase throughout the Early Access period. However, they may eventually be replaced by other DLC offerings.

We’ll be sharing more details about our development roadmap and our current priorities on our social channels and as part of regular update shows.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“SMITE 2 is currently in Open Beta phase, Free-to-Play for all players to enjoy!

The SMITE 2 Alpha period built a strong foundation of a game that we believe has become truly fun to play and already better than SMITE 1 in many areas. Now in Open Beta phase, we aim to make continuous improvements, opening the door for new gameplay and tech innovations.

We want to build upon that foundation with a core group of early adopter players, who choose to take part in Early Access. Please share your feedback with us via social media, in-game surveys and just playing the game so we can make a truly great SMITE experience.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“SMITE 2 is now available Free-to-Play! While the Founder’s Editions will no longer gate access to SMITE 2, they will still be available for purchase.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We believe in a community driven approach to game development. During the Alphas we’ve already been gathering and analyzing your feedback via social media, surveys, Discord and more, and continue to keep this front of mind when making changes to SMITE 2.

Our official Discord server, linked from the game client, offers a place to leave feedback, and we will also be checking feedback here in our Steam discussion forums, and wherever else we can find public discussion about the game. We expect to make many changes and improvements inspired by our player community, and will discuss these in our dev showcase streams and other social content, as well as in depth in the patch notes for each update.

Additionally, players may improve this game simply by playing, as anonymous gameplay data is transmitted to us, which we utilize for things like balance adjustments of individual gods based on their real win/loss rates. A lot of this data is also publicly visible via partner stat tracker sites. The combination of our impressions as a development team, feedback we're hearing from our player community, and gameplay data we're collecting from players all should help us form a more-complete picture of how we can make SMITE 2 the best it can be.”

About This Game

Fight as a God

Choose your God from across mythology and wield unique godly abilities. Hurl bolts of lightning as Zeus, master the arcane powers of death as Anubis, or backstab your foes as Loki.

The Third-Person Action MOBA

No other game offers combat that feels like your favorite hero shooter, crossed with the deep strategy you can only find in a 5v5 MOBA. Feel the impact of every shot, sneak up on enemies, and combo abilities with teammates to take down the enemy Titan in Conquest mode, or defeat waves of minions and enemy Gods to secure victory in Arena mode.

Play Anywhere, With Anyone

SMITE 2 features full cross-play and cross-progression, so you can play with friends regardless of platform and take your unlocks with you.

SMITE, Reforged

More than 40 million players have enjoyed SMITE. Now the next generation of action MOBA begins, with immersive visuals powered by Unreal Engine 5, more impactful combat, and new creative gameplay options like playstyle-redefining Aspects to shape the game in your own way.

Minutes to Learn, a Lifetime to Master

SMITE 2 is the easiest MOBA to start playing, with features like auto-build and auto-level so you focus on fighting. But the full depth of MOBA gameplay awaits, letting you find your own path to victory across item building, map control, tactical brilliance, or simply outplaying your foes. Compete in a variety of game modes with the right complexity for your mood: The deathmatch-like 5v5 Arena mode, 3v3 single-lane Joust, 5v5 random character Assault, the classic 5v5 three-lane Conquest, and Ranked Conquest, where you’ll climb the leaderboards and become a true god in the SMITE 2 community.

Great Rewards Await

If you played SMITE, your Divine Legacy is waiting for you in SMITE 2. You’ll have access to amazing cross-gen skins, badges and Legacy Gems after you link your SMITE account.
